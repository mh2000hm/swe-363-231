About Us



Our Story

The idea for My Flashcards began as we searched for a way to make studying more engaging, effective, and most importantly fun for students. However, our vision for our website does not just aim at gamifying the process of studying, but it extends to helping students achieve results. Therefore, we knew from the beginning that we must adhere to the feedback of students and hold ourselves to high academic standards.

The following table lists sample fields My Flashcards would support:
Field Student Type Suitable Majors Example Question
Chemistry Undergraduate CHEM - CE Name the following compound
Physics Undergraduate PHYS - ME According to Ohm's law, when electrical resistance increases what happens to the voltage?
Computer Science Undergraduate CS - SWE Describe what the following command does.

Mission Statement

"To help students learn using interactive tools from a compiled collection of study materials built by students for students."
